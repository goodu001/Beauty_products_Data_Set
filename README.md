
# Beauty_products_Data_Set

This synthetic dataset was generated by a large language model (LLM) for use in Power BI visualization practice.The dataset contains six tables covering product data and operational workflows.

1. beauty_calendar_modified.csv
2. beauty_customers_modified.csv
3. beauty_order_items.csv
4. beauty_orders.csv
5. beauty_products.csv
6. beauty_stores.csv

### Relationships

beauty_calendar_modified[Date] → beauty_orders[OrderDate]
*1 ── * (single-direction: Calendar filters Orders)

beauty_customers_modified[CustomerID] → beauty_orders[CustomerID]
*1 ── * (Customers filter Orders)

beauty_stores[StoreID] → beauty_orders[StoreID]
*1 ── * (Stores filter Orders)

beauty_orders[OrderID] → beauty_order_items[OrderID]
*1 ── * (Orders filter Order Items)

beauty_products[ProductID] → beauty_order_items[ProductID]
*1 ── * (Products filter Order Items)

Propagation note: Calendar/Customers/Stores → Orders → Order_Items. Products only filter Order_Items (no direct Products ↔ Orders link). All shown links are active 1-to-many, single filter direction from dimension → fact.

### Page 1 - Beauty Product Overview

**KPI: TOTAL SALES (0.59M):** Single value card showing overall sales across the current filter context.

**Top sale by Category (Sum of LineTotal):** Vertical bar chart comparing category revenue. Leaders: Skincare (~0.15M), Face (~0.08M), Hair (~0.08M), Eyes (~0.05M).

**Sum of OrderTotal by City:** Horizontal bar chart ranking cities (e.g., Singapore, Johor Bahru, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Kuala..., Khon..., Bangkok). Shows where demand concentrates geographically.

**Sum of OrderTotal by Year, Quarter and Month:** Time-series view (Jan 2024 to Jan 2026) for seasonality and growth/slowdowns over months.

**Top 10 Spenders:** Bar chart of Count of OrderID by CustomerID (e.g., 77, 1007, 66, 860), highlighting who purchases most frequently.

**Top sale by StoreName (Sum of OrderTotal):** Compares outlets (e.g., BeautyHub #03, #01, #06, #02) to find top-performing stores (scale shown up to ~50K).

**OrderTotal by Gender:** Bars for Female (~0.33M), Male (~0.24M), Non-binary (~0.02M), Prefer not to say (~0.01M); understand buyer mix.

**OrderTotal by Channel:** Bars for Street Boutique, Outlet, Warehouse, Mall; compares revenue by selling channel.

**Table: Beauty Product Overview:** ProductName with total sales, e.g., Aurora Glow Eyeliner, Face Wash, Lip Gloss; item-level contribution checks.

**Slicers:** Country and City to filter all visuals together.
